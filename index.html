Locally owned and operated since 1972, Arnold's Market carries choice cuts of meat, organic, local, and gourmet items, along with everyday staples. Arnold's friendly and knowledgeable staff ensures customers always get the best cuts of meat, quality produce, and can assist you with special requests and orders.

Keeping it local isn't just a catch phrase for us; we support our local neighbourhoods and our local producers. Our global selection of groceries is complemented by numerous local flavors-from meats and cheeses to fresh produce and a fine selection of brews and wines.
